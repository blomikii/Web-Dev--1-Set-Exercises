Mango Graham Float

Indulge in the irresistible flavors of ripe mangoes, creamy whipped cream, and crunchy graham crackers layered to perfection. Mango Graham Float is a delightful dessert that brings the essence of summer to your palate with every heavenly bite. Experience the tropical bliss in this simple yet delectable treat—a true celebration of sun-kissed days and sweet memories.

It is basically made out of grahams and all-purpose cream or whipped cream that is made into a cake, and is topped with ripe mangoes, appearing to be floating on ‘clouds’. The best part about this is you get to skip the baking and the cooking!

Ingredients

  • 3-4 Ripe Mangoes
  • 200 grams Graham Crackers
  • 1 Can Condensed Milk
  • 2 Cups All Purpose Cream

Instructions

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine the chilled all-purpose cream and sweetened condensed milk. Mix until well combined and smooth. This will be your cream mixture.
  2. Prepare a rectangular or square dish that will fit the layers of graham crackers and cream mixture. You can also use individual serving glasses for a more elegant presentation.
  3. Spread a thin layer of the cream mixture at the bottom of the dish.
  4. Arrange a layer of graham crackers on top of the cream mixture, covering the entire bottom of the dish.
  5. Add another layer of the cream mixture on top of the graham crackers, spreading it evenly.
  6. Arrange a layer of sliced mangoes on top of the cream mixture, covering it completely.
  7. Repeat the layers until you reach the desired height or use up all the ingredients, ending with a layer of cream mixture on top.
  8. Sprinkle crushed graham crackers on top for added texture and garnish with mango chunks if desired.
  9. Cover the dish with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 4 hours or overnight to allow the flavors to meld and the dessert to set.
  10. Once chilled and set, slice into squares or scoop into individual serving glasses.
